BMW of Jackson — Sales Opportunity Brief

Internal strategy draft · cleaned prospect view · focused on the real operating story

Main Read

BMW of Jackson appears to have a real internet conversion problem. Even after cleaning the export down to the likely unique prospect pool, internet still dominates the lead mix, but it produces far less sold output than walk-in and store-driven business. The opportunity is likely not more lead volume first — it is better internet lead handling, cleaner qualification, and tighter follow-up process.

Data note: the original export had repeated client records. A first-pass dedupe reduced the file from 441 rows to 346 cleaned prospect records. All analysis below uses the cleaned counts.

Main Opportunity Angles

  • Internet conversion is the real wedge.
  • Bad-lead volume is still too high even after cleanup.
  • Store-driven business looks much healthier than cold internet demand.
  • Management probably needs cleaner source and rep visibility to know where to coach.
