Current Strengths

  • Demand is there. Even after cleanup, the store now shows an even larger usable prospect pool.
  • Internet conversion improved from the earlier baseline. The store is not stuck at the prior low-water mark.
  • There is still active pipeline to work. The cleaned file continues to show a meaningful amount of live opportunity.

Near-Term Opportunities

  • Internet lead conversion still needs work. It improved, but it is still the biggest likely operating opportunity.
  • Duplicate volume is still heavy. That makes the working pipeline look less clean than it should.
  • Source-quality visibility is still needed. Management needs to know which sources are productive versus noisy or repetitive.

Recommended Management Actions

  1. Review internet lead handling first. That is still where the largest share of opportunity sits.
  2. Build a source-quality and duplicate scorecard. Management should review cleaned source volume, duplicate volume, and sold output weekly.
  3. Separate bad leads and duplicate chains from workable leads more aggressively. This will sharpen pipeline focus.
  4. Use this May cleaned file as the new baseline. Future process improvements should be measured against this updated snapshot.
